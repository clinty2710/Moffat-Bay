Our History


Moffat Bay Lodge has a rich and storied history that dates back to the early 20th century. Founded by John Moffat, a visionary entrepreneur and nature enthusiast, the lodge began as a modest lakeside retreat for those seeking solace in the beauty of nature.

Over the years, Moffat Bay Lodge evolved into a premier destination for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a wide range of recreational activities including boating, fishing, hiking, and wildlife watching. The lodge's commitment to preserving the natural environment while providing exceptional guest experiences has been the cornerstone of its success.
